General Information

Get started with Khronium, a simple personal manager, which comes with three built in modules. These modules will allow everyone to administer their personal time, multiple projects and to take care of their mind and exhaustion. Read below to find out more.

Time Management

With the time management module you will be able to create and keep track of multiple tasks on a daily basis.

Project Management

The project manager will provide anybody with boards which allows the creation of multiple custom lists and tasks to faccilitate your needs.

Mind and Exhaustion Management

The mind and exhaustion management module will provide users with two forms. The first one will analyze how depressed you are through a scale called the "Montgomery–Åsberg Depression Rating Scale". The latter will provide you with another scale named "Karolinska Exhaustion Disorder Scale" which will analyze how exhausted you are. Both of them will provide you with the feedback and guidance to resolve these problems.
